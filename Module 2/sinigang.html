Sinigang: A Comforting Sour Soup

Sinigang is a tangy soup known for its refreshing sourness, typically derived from tamarind. It is a staple comfort food, especially during rainy days.

Sinigang is a traditional Filipino soup known for its distinct sour and refreshing flavor. This comforting dish features tender meat or seafood cooked with tamarind, tomatoes, and fresh vegetables, resulting in a warm and satisfying bowl enjoyed across the Philippines.
- Chef Pateña, Filipino gooner

Preparation Steps:

  1. Boil meat with tomatoes until tender.
  2. Add tamarind and vegetables.
  3. Season with fish sauce to taste.
  4. Simmer briefly and serve hot.

Cooking Tips

  • Adjust sourness gradually to achieve balance.

Ingredients :

  • Pork Belly or Shrimp: 500 g
  • Tamarind Paste or Fresh Tamarind: ¼ cup
  • Tomatoes: 2, quartered
  • Radish: 1 cup, sliced
  • Water Spinach (Kangkong): 1 bunch
