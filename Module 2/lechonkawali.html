Lechon Kawali: Crispy Pork Perfection

Lechon Kawali is prized for its contrast between ultra-crispy skin and juicy meat, commonly served with liver sauce or spiced vinegar.

"Lechon Kawali is a popular Filipino pork dish famous for its crispy skin and juicy interior. This dish involves boiling pork belly until tender, then deep-frying it to golden perfection, delivering a satisfying contrast of textures in every bite."
- Chef Bautista, Filipino gooner

Preparation Steps:

  1. Boil pork with garlic and bay leaves until tender.
  2. Air-dry or chill thoroughly.
  3. Deep-fry until golden and crisp.
  4. Chop and serve immediately.

Cooking Tips

  • Drying the pork completely is key to achieving crisp skin.

Ingredients :

  • Pork Belly: 1 kg
  • Garlic: 4 cloves
  • Bay Leaves: 2
  • Salt and Pepper: 1 pinch
